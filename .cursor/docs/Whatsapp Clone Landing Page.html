Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need the official WhatsApp Business API?

No! We offer two modes: Performance Mode (works with any WhatsApp number) and Stable Mode (uses official API). You can start immediately with your existing number.

Can my WhatsApp number get banned?

We implement strict anti-spam measures and rate limiting. However, if you send spam or violate WhatsApp's terms, any number can be banned. We provide best practices guidance to keep your account safe.

How long does setup take?

About 5 minutes! Scan a QR code, enter your website URL, and our AI does the rest. You can send your first automated message within 10 minutes.

Do you store my WhatsApp messages?

Yes, messages are stored securely and encrypted. You maintain full control and can export or delete your data anytime. We're GDPR compliant.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely! No contracts, no commitments. Cancel with one click from your dashboard.

Beta users get 60 days free access to all Pro features. After launch, we'll offer a 14-day free trial.
